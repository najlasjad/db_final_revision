Course Recommendation Analysis

📚✨ This Course Recommendation Analysis system helps students plan their semester wisely by analyzing course combinations. It uses the 🧩 Apriori algorithm and past enrollment data to check if two courses should be taken together. It looks at difficulty levels (for example, Hard) and average grades 📊 to make a recommendation. If both courses are hard, it will suggest not to take them together, which helps students have a better learning experience. 🚦✅ This system also helps academic advisors and departments make better plans for students. 🎓📝

Attendance Prediction

📊✨ The Attendance Prediction page helps students estimate their attendance percentage for a particular course based on past academic records. 📝👨‍🎓 Students just need to fill in their name, previous average assignment score, and previous average semester grade, and select the course and semester they plan to take. After clicking "Predict Attendance," the system will calculate and display their predicted attendance as a percentage with a bar chart. 💻🔢 This feature helps students plan better and understand their learning progress. 🎯📈
